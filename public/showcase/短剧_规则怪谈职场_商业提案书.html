Core Contradiction核心矛盾

Rules are both oppressor and oppressed. Humans are both victims and feed for the rules.
This is a parasitic relationship: rules grow from human behavior (#41 Rule Cultivation Period), humans survive through rule loopholes (#17 Quota Carryover). The true tension: neither side can kill the other — because killing the other means suicide.

规则既是压迫者，也是被压迫者。人类既是规则的受害者，也是规则的养料。
这是寄生关系：规则从人类行为中生长（#41 规则培育期），人类通过规则漏洞求生——17 额度结转）。真正的张力在于：任何一方都无法杀死对方——因为杀死对方等于自杀。
